## 📖 Overview
**Forexa Pro** is a next-generation decision-support terminal that merges traditional technical analysis with modern alternative data. By combining high-frequency technical indicators with real-time **Sentiment Analysis**, the system filters market noise to provide high-conviction trading signals.

> [!TIP]
> **The Confluence Logic:** Forexa doesn't just look at price. It ensures technical signals (RSI/Volume) are backed by human fundamental conviction (NLP Sentiment) before issuing a signal.

---

## 🧠 Core Model Architecture
The engine running in the **[Google Colab](https://colab.research.google.com/drive/1WSM1h2ovRp-vwfXgdRIbInh_Hs1AvE6V)** utilizes a multi-layered AI approach:

### 1. LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) 🕒
* **Role:** Time-Series Forecasting.
* **Function:** A Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) designed to process sequences of data. It identifies long-term price patterns and "remembers" historical volatility to predict the next candle's direction.

### 2. XGBoost (Extreme Gradient Boosting) ⚡
* **Role:** Signal Classification.
* **Function:** Analyzes the current market state (Spread, Volume, RSI) to classify high-probability outcomes into three states: `BUY`, `SELL`, or `WAIT`.

### 3. NLP Sentiment Engine 📰
* **Role:** Psychological Filtering.
* **Function:** A specialized Natural Language Processing model that scans live financial news and social feeds. It assigns a polarity score to market mood, providing a "sanity check" for technical indicators.
